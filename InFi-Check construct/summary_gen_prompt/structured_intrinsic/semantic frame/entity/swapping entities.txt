Error Type: Intrinsic/Semantic Frame/Entity
Method: Swapping entities
Few Shot: No
Instruction: 
1. Select a random entity in the summary.
2. Replace the selected entity in the summary with another entity in the document.
Format:
- "original text in summary": The original sentence with the selected entity, and the sentence with the entity for replacement.
- "chosen element": The chosen entity in original text.
- "modification explanation": Description of the modification.
- "modified element": The new entity used to replace the chosen entity.
- "modified text": The full sentence after the modification.
- "explanation": A clear explanation of how the meaning of the original text has been altered.
- "full text of modified summary": The full text of the modified summary. Split the summary by sentence into a list.
- "wrong information": Point out the specific wrong information introduced in the summary after the modification.
