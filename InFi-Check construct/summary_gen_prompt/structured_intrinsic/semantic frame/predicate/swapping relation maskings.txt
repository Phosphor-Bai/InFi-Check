Error Type: Intrinsic/Semantic Frame/Predicate
Method: Swapping relation maskings
Few Shot: No
Instruction: 
1. Select a dependency relation in the summary.
2. Replace the predicate with a wrong predicate, making the meaning of the sentence differs greatly from the origin one. Modification to the sentence containing the dependency relation is allowed for consistency.
Format:
- "original text in summary": The original sentence or sentences with the selected dependency relation.
- "chosen element": The chosen predicate in original text.
- "modification explanation": Description of the modification.
- "modified element": The new predicate used to replace the chosen predicate.
- "modified text": The full sentence after the modification.
- "explanation": A clear explanation of how the meaning of the original text has been altered.
- "full text of modified summary": The full text of the modified summary. Split the summary by sentence into a list.
- "wrong information": Point out the specific wrong information introduced in the summary after the modification.
